# Creative and Copy Audit

**Date:** 2026-03-13
**Campaign:** mtd_branding_leads_06mar26
**Active Ad:** your_brand_looks (carousel, 6 cards)

---

## Executive Summary

The creative execution is above average for a small agency. The carousel follows a clear PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solution) narrative arc across 6 cards, the primary text is well-structured, and the visual identity is consistent. However, the creative strategy has structural issues: only 1 active ad (no testing), the CTA button choice is wrong, and the ad-to-landing-page message match is weak. The creative is doing its job of generating interest (6,010 impressions, visible engagement), but the post-click experience fails to convert.

---

## 1. Carousel Card Analysis

### Card 1: Hook
- **Headline:** "Nobody Knows What You Do"
- **Description:** "Your brand looks fine, but fine doesn't convert."
- **URL:** https://milktreeagency.com/
- **Assessment:** Strong hook. Provocative, pattern-interrupting. "Fine doesn't convert" is memorable. GOOD.

### Card 2: Agitation
- **Headline:** "10 People. 10 Different Answers."
- **Description:** "Your visuals, messaging and positioning are all saying different things."
- **Assessment:** Good agitation. Makes the abstract problem tangible. The "10 people test" is a concrete diagnostic the reader can verify. GOOD.

### Card 3: Reframe
- **Headline:** "Clarity Converts. Everything Else Is Decoration."
- **Description:** "This isn't a design problem. It's a strategy problem."
- **Assessment:** Elevates from tactical (design) to strategic (positioning). Positions Milktree as strategic, not just "logo makers". GOOD.

### Card 4: Solution
- **Headline:** "A Complete Brand System"
- **Description:** "Positioning. Messaging. Visual identity. Content. UX. All aligned."
- **Assessment:** Clear solution framing. Lists specific deliverables. Could benefit from a specific result ("so your ideal customer gets it in 5 seconds"). ADEQUATE.

### Card 5: Social Proof
- **Headline:** "200+ Brands Built. The Results Speak."
- **Description:** "250% avg. enquiry increase. GBP 1M+ ad spend managed."
- **Assessment:** Strong credibility. However "GBP 1M+ ad spend managed" is an odd flex for a branding agency. Mixing brand + performance metrics dilutes the positioning. MIXED.

### Card 6: CTA
- **Headline:** "Book Your Free Brand Audit"
- **Description:** "We'll audit your positioning, messaging and design and tell you what to fix first."
- **Assessment:** Clear offer with specificity. "Tell you what to fix first" sets expectations. CTA matches primary text closing. GOOD.

### Carousel Arc Rating: 7/10
The narrative arc is well-constructed (hook > agitate > reframe > solve > prove > convert). Main weaknesses: Card 4 is generic, Card 5 mixes brand/performance claims.

## 2. Primary Text Analysis

```
Ask 10 people what your business does.
You'll get 10 different answers.

That's not a branding problem. It's a revenue problem.
And no amount of new colours or fonts will fix it.

Milktree builds brand systems that align your positioning,
messaging and design so your ideal customer gets it instantly.

200+ brands. 250% avg. enquiry increase. GBP 1M+ ad spend managed.

Book a free audit. We'll show you exactly what to fix first.
```

### Strengths:
- Strong opening hook (matches carousel card 2)
- Problem-to-revenue reframe in paragraph 2 elevates urgency
- Clear CTA at the end
- Social proof embedded naturally
- Under Meta's 125-character "see more" threshold for first line

### Weaknesses:
- **No audience targeting in copy** [HIGH] - Doesn't say "If you're a business owner..." or "For B2B companies who..." Cold traffic needs to self-qualify in the first 2 lines.
- **"GBP 1M+ ad spend managed" again** [MEDIUM] - Confusing for a branding service ad. Is this a brand agency or a media agency?
- **No specific outcome promise** [MEDIUM] - "Gets it instantly" is vague. Compare: "so they enquire within 48 hours of finding you"
- **No urgency/scarcity** [LOW] - No reason to act now vs. later

### Primary Text Rating: 6/10

## 3. Advantage+ Creative Text Variations

5 AI-generated variations are enabled with "+3.85% CTR opportunity". Variations include:

1. "10 people, 10 different answers about what your business does?"
2. Longer version with bullet points (200+ brands, 250% enquiry increase, GBP 1M+ ad spend)
3. "Ask 10 people what your business does, get 10 different answers? That's not a branding issue, it's a revenue killer!"
4. "Is your brand message getting lost in translation?"
5. "Got a branding problem? Ask 10 people what your business does, get 10 different answers?"

**Assessment:** "Tailor variations to personas" is OFF. Should be ON for Advantage+ targeting to match text to audience segments. The AI variations are reasonable rewrites but all use the same angle. Would benefit from entirely different hooks (social proof lead, outcome lead, fear lead).

## 4. CTA Button

**Selected:** "Book now"

### 4.1 Wrong CTA Button [HIGH]

"Book now" implies calendar commitment and transaction. For a free brand audit offer, better options:
- "Learn more" (lowest friction, best for cold traffic awareness)
- "Sign up" (if leading to a form)
- "Get offer" (emphasises the free audit)

**"Book now" is the highest-commitment CTA Meta offers.** For cold traffic that has never heard of Milktree, this creates maximum friction. Combined with the landing page having no form above the fold, the user clicks "Book now" expecting a booking flow and instead sees a long-scroll portfolio website.

## 5. Visual Assessment

### Card Design
- Dark background (near-black) with yellow/gold accent colours
- Clean typography, legible at mobile sizes
- Consistent visual identity across all 6 cards
- 1440x1440 square format (correct for carousel)

### Strengths:
- Brand consistency is excellent
- High contrast text is readable
- Professional but not corporate
- Colour scheme stands out in feed

### Weaknesses:
- **No human faces** [HIGH] - Ads with faces consistently outperform faceless ads by 20-30% on Meta. All 6 cards are text + graphic overlays. No founder photo, no client testimonials with faces, no team shots.
- **All cards look similar** [MEDIUM] - Dark background + text overlay x6 creates visual monotony. By card 3-4, users stop swiping.
- **No before/after** [MEDIUM] - For a brand transformation service, showing a before/after (old brand vs new brand) would be more compelling than abstract statements.

## 6. Format Display Options

| Option | Status | Assessment |
|--------|--------|------------|
| Show cards as collection tiles | Enabled | OK |
| Show cards as single media | Enabled | Risky - may show Card 4 or Card 5 without narrative context |
| Multi-advertiser ads | Enabled | Should DISABLE - your ad appears alongside competitor ads |

### 6.1 Multi-Advertiser Ads Enabled [HIGH]

When multi-advertiser is on, Meta can show your ad alongside competitor ads in the same ad unit. For a branding agency, being shown next to cheaper competitors directly undercuts the positioning.

**Recommendation:** Disable multi-advertiser ads immediately.

## 7. Ad-to-Landing-Page Message Match

| Ad Element | Landing Page Element | Match? |
|------------|---------------------|--------|
| "Nobody Knows What You Do" | "Your brand should sell for you." | NO |
| "10 People. 10 Different Answers." | Not on landing page | NO |
| "Book Your Free Brand Audit" | "Book Your Free Brand Audit" (hero CTA) | YES |
| PAS narrative | Problem section exists but different angle | PARTIAL |
| Dark + yellow brand colours | Dark + yellow theme | YES |

### 7.1 Weak Message Match [CRITICAL]

The ad's hook ("Nobody Knows What You Do" / "10 People. 10 Different Answers.") is completely absent from the landing page. A user who clicked because they related to "10 people, 10 different answers" lands on a page that says "Your brand should sell for you" - a different message entirely.

**Recommendation:** Landing page hero headline should echo the ad hook. Either change the LP headline to match, or create a dedicated LP for this ad with matching language.

---

## 8. Creative Testing Status

### 8.1 No Active Testing [CRITICAL]

- 4 ads exist but 3 are turned off
- No A/B creative test running
- No variation in angles (all use the "10 people" hook)
- Advantage+ creative enhancements partially enabled (4/6)

**Recommendation:**
1. Re-enable at least 2 additional ad variations
2. Test different angles: social proof lead, outcome/transformation lead, competitor comparison
3. Test formats: static image, video (even a simple motion graphic), single image with testimonial
4. Test different hooks: before/after case study, specific stat lead ("257 leads from GBP 840")
